How it works

From prompt to production in three moves.

A team of specialized agents debates the plan before writing a single line of code.

01 / Describe

Say what you want.

Natural language. Full-stack. "A marketplace for used books with Stripe checkout." The planner clarifies, never guesses.

02 / Critique

The team argues.

Team-Lead and Critic agents red-team the plan before execution. Bad ideas die early, not in production.

03 / Ship

Deploy to the edge.

Coder agents write files in parallel. Tester runs sandbox checks. One click → live on Cloudflare.

Questions

Frequently asked.

Straight answers. No asterisks.

How is this different from bolt.new or v0?

Those are mostly frontend code generators. VibeSDK ships full-stack apps — database, auth, background jobs, emails, webhooks — wired to production infra on first try. And you see every agent working, not just the output.

Can I use my own Anthropic / OpenAI / Gemini keys?

Yes. Every tier supports BYO keys. They're encrypted in a per-user Durable Object with XChaCha20-Poly1305, rotatable, and never visible in logs.

What stack does the generated app use?

React 19 + Vite + Tailwind frontend. Cloudflare Workers backend. D1 or Postgres. You pick a template or let the Planner choose based on your prompt.

Is the platform itself open source?

Yes. Apache 2.0. Deploy your own instance to your own Cloudflare account with one click. Generated apps are yours — no runtime lock-in.

What happens when I hit my free limit?

Your existing apps keep running. New generations pause until the monthly reset, or you can upgrade to Pro for 100/mo and 4× parallel agents.

Do you train on my prompts or code?

No. Your sessions are yours. We don't send your code to any training pipeline. BYO-keys mode means your data doesn't even touch our LLM bills.
