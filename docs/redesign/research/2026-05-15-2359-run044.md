## 4. Replit May 2026 — ChatGPT Integration + Effort-Based Pricing

**Source:** Replit blog

### ChatGPT integration
- Build in ChatGPT → open in Replit to publish
- "Your app stays in the ChatGPT conversation"
- Distribution play: Replit embedded in ChatGPT usage
- **Assessment:** LOW threat to vibesdk. Different channel (ChatGPT users → Replit, not vibesdk's India-dev-first audience). Validates that AI builders are becoming distribution layers, not just tools.

- Simple changes: < $0.25 / checkpoint (unchanged)

- **Assessment:** Opaque pricing (effort-based = variable cost). vibesdk flat ₹1,699/mo is a COUNTER-NARRATIVE positioning — "know your costs, no surprise bills." Highlight in pricing page.

### Private publishing expanded
- Now on Core + Starter plans (was Pro+ only)
- vibesdk: projects served from DO + sandbox; no explicit private/public toggle — N/A for now
